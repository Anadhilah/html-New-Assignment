Ghana ia a developing country in the African continent specifically the western part. It has sixteen regions and seven climatic or physio-graphic feateures which are
Gambaga escarpment, Akwapim Togo range, Coastal plains, Voltarian Sandstone basine Kwehu Mampong Scarp, Forest dissected plateau and the Savana plains.

Ghana is a democratic country that practices momnocephalous or presidential syatem of Ghana which is both ceremonial functions and real executive
powers are in the had of one person known as the president.
